# Mass Effect 2 — Research & Upgrades Reference

Complete research tree with costs and effects. Referenced from [SPEC.md §6](../SPEC.md).

All research is conducted at the **Tech Lab** on the Normandy CIC Deck (available after recruiting Mordin).

---

## Weapon Damage Upgrades

Each tier provides **+10% damage**. Tiers 6–7 may require DLC content.

| Upgrade | Weapon Type | Mineral | Tier Costs |
|---------|-----------|---------|------------|
| Kinetic Pulsar | Assault Rifles | Iridium | 2,500 / 5,000 / 7,500 / 10,000 / 12,500 (5–7 tiers) |
| Titan Pulsar | Heavy Pistols | Palladium | 2,500 / 5,000 / 7,500 / 10,000 (4–6 tiers) |
| Synchronized Pulsar | Shotguns | Platinum | 2,500 / 5,000 / 7,500 / 10,000 (4–6 tiers) |

| Microfield Pulsar | SMGs | Iridium | 2,500 / 5,000 / 7,500 / 10,000 (4–6 tiers) |

## Weapon Special Upgrades

Unlock after researching 2–3 base damage tiers for the corresponding weapon type.

### Penetration Upgrades (+50% vs specified defense type)

| Upgrade | Weapon Type | Effect | Cost |
|---------|-----------|--------|------|
| Tungsten Jacket | Assault Rifles | +25% vs Armor/Shields/Barriers | 15,000 Iridium |
| Sabot Jacketing | Heavy Pistols | +50% vs Armor | 15,000 Palladium |
| Microphasic Pulse | Shotguns | +50% vs Shields/Barriers* | 15,000 Platinum |
| Tungsten Sabot Jacket | Sniper Rifles | +50% vs Armor | 15,000 Platinum |
| Phasic Jacketing | SMGs | +50% vs Shields/Barriers | 15,000 Iridium |

*Shotgun Microphasic Pulse is bugged: does NOT apply to Barriers.

### Advanced Upgrades

| Upgrade | Weapon Type | Effect | Cost |
|---------|-----------|--------|------|
| Targeting VI | Assault Rifles | Squad accuracy boost | 25,000 Iridium |
| Smart Rounds | Heavy Pistols | Chance for double damage | 25,000 Palladium |
| Thermal Sink | Shotguns | Double magazine capacity | 25,000 Platinum |
| Combat Scanner | Sniper Rifles | +50% headshot damage (multiplicative) | 25,000 Platinum |
| Heat Sink Capacity | SMGs | +50% magazine size | 25,000 Iridium |
