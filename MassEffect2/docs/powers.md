# Mass Effect 2 — Powers Reference

Complete power list with evolution branches, cooldowns, and damage values. Referenced from [SPEC.md §4](../SPEC.md).

Platform: Original ME2 (2010). Legendary Edition differences noted where known.

---

## Power Rank Costs

All powers follow the same point progression:
- Rank 1: 1 squad point
- Rank 2: 2 squad points
- Rank 3: 3 squad points
- Rank 4: 4 squad points (choose between two permanent evolutions)
- **Total per power: 10 squad points**

## Unique Squadmate Powers

### Shadow Strike (Kasumi Unique)

Available to: Kasumi Goto only

- Recharge: 6.00s
- Kasumi cloaks, teleports behind the target, delivers a high-damage melee strike, then returns to her original position
- Bypasses cover; guaranteed hit on targeted enemy
- Damage increases with rank; can stagger unprotected targets

| Rank | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 1–3 | Increasing damage |
| 4a: — | (Kasumi does not have a rank 4 evolution for Shadow Strike; it is replaced by her loyalty power Flashbang Grenade) |

## Biotic Powers

### Warp

Available to: Adept, Sentinel; Miranda, Thane, Samara/Morinth, Liara (DLC)

- Recharge: 6.00s (Shepard), 12.00s (squadmates)
- 75% of damage dealt upfront, 25% as DoT
- 2× damage vs Barriers and Armor; 0.5× vs Shields
- Stops health regeneration
- Detonates biotic combos (Pull/Singularity targets)

|------|--------|

## Tech Powers

### Overload

Available to: Engineer, Sentinel; Garrus, Kasumi, Miranda

- Recharge: 6.00s (Shepard), 12.00s (squadmates)
- 2× listed damage vs Shields; listed damage vs synthetic health, barriers, armor
- Does NO damage to organic health
- Rank 2+: stuns unprotected synthetics for 3.00s
- Rank 3+: overheats enemy weapons for 6.00s

| Rank | Damage | Radius |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | 120 | 1.20m |
| 2 | 140 | 1.20m |
| 3 | 160 | 1.20m |
| 4a: Heavy Overload | 200, synthetics explode on death | 1.20m |
| 4b: Area Overload | 160 | 3.00m |
