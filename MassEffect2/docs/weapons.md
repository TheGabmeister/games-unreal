# Mass Effect 2 — Weapons Reference

Complete weapon list with stats and special properties. Referenced from [SPEC.md §6](../SPEC.md).

Platform: Original ME2 (2010). Base damage values from coalesced.ini extraction (community-verified). Legendary Edition values may differ.

---

## Weapon Category Proficiencies

| Category | Available To |
|----------|-------------|
| Heavy Pistols | All classes |
| Submachine Guns | All except Soldier |
| Assault Rifles | Soldier (others via Advanced Weapon Training) |
| Shotguns | Soldier, Vanguard (others via training) |
| Sniper Rifles | Soldier, Infiltrator (others via training) |
| Heavy Weapons | All classes |

**Advanced Weapon Training** grants ONE additional weapon type during the Collector Ship mission.

## Weapon vs Defense Type Multipliers

Base multipliers before penetration research upgrades:

| Weapon Type | vs Armor | vs Shields | vs Barriers |
|-------------|---------|-----------|------------|
| Heavy Pistols | 1.5× | 1.0× | 1.0× |
| SMGs (Tempest) | 1.0× | 1.5× | 1.5× |
| Assault Rifles | 1.25× | 1.25× | 1.25× |
| Shotguns | 1.0× | 1.5× | 1.5× |
| Sniper (Mantis) | 1.5× | 1.0× | 1.0× |
| Sniper (Viper) | 1.35× | 1.15× | 1.15× |

With penetration research upgrades (+50% vs specified type):

| Weapon Type | vs Armor (upgraded) | vs Shields (upgraded) | vs Barriers (upgraded) |
|-------------|--------------------|--------------------|----------------------|
| Heavy Pistols | 2.25× | 1.0× | 1.0× |
| SMGs | 1.0× | 2.25× | 2.25× |
| Shotguns | 1.0× | 2.25× | 1.5×* |
| Snipers (Mantis) | 2.25× | 1.0× | 1.0× |

*Shotgun penetration vs Barriers is bugged and does NOT apply.

**Note on AR upgrades**: Assault rifle damage upgrades are multiplicative with base damage (unique among weapon types; all others are additive).

## Squadmate Weapon Behavior

- Squadmate weapon damage: 40–65% of Shepard's base damage (varies by weapon type)
- Near-perfect accuracy (machine-like tracking) but cannot score headshots
- Cannot proc the pistol double-damage effect
- Squad ordered-attack command: +50% damage bonus on targeted attack
- Not affected by weapon recoil
