# Mass Effect 2 — Armor Reference

All armor pieces, full sets, stat bonuses, and acquisition sources. Referenced from [SPEC.md §6](../SPEC.md).

---

## Armor System Overview

Shepard's armor has 5 customizable slots: **Helmet, Chest, Shoulders, Arms, Legs**. Each piece provides specific stat bonuses.

**Full armor sets** replace all 5 pieces with a unified appearance and combined bonuses. Pieces from different sets cannot be mixed (exception: Kestrel Armor).

Armor is cosmetic-only for squadmates — no stat bonuses.

| Collector Armor | +10% Health Regen, +10% Storm Speed, +20% Health | Collector's Edition DLC |
| Inferno Armor | +10% Paragon/Renegade Bonus, +10% Power Damage, +10% Storm Speed | DLC |
| Terminus Assault Armor | +10% Storm Speed, +15% Shield Strength, +10% Ammo Reserve | Terminus DLC |
| Kestrel Armor | +30% Melee, +5% Headshot, +11% Weapon Dmg, +25% Shield, +5% Heavy Ammo | Aegis Pack DLC |

**Kestrel Armor** is unique: its 5 pieces can be individually swapped with other custom armor pieces.
