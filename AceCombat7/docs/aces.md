# Ace Combat 7 — Named Aces

24 hidden named aces across the campaign. Referenced from [SPEC.md §9.2](../SPEC.md).

**Spawn rules:**
- Normal difficulty or higher only
- Only when replaying missions (after first campaign completion)
- Each has a unique trigger condition within its mission

Shooting down all 24 unlocks the "Bird of Prey" achievement and the X-02S Strike Wyvern Special skin.
