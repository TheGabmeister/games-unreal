# Ace Combat 7 — Special Weapon Catalog

Complete weapon descriptions and mechanics. Referenced from [SPEC.md §1.4](../SPEC.md).

---

## Air-to-Air Missiles

| Abbrev | Full Name | Damage | Behavior |
|--------|-----------|--------|----------|
| **HPAA** | High-Power Air-to-Air Missile | 160 | Highest damage A2A missile. Poor homing, slow reload. Fires in salvos of 2. Can one-shot kill with armor parts. |
| **HVAA** | Hyper-Velocity Air-to-Air Missile | High | Extremely fast projectile, long range. Poor turning radius. Best for head-on or surprise attacks. |
| **QAAM** | Quick Maneuver Air-to-Air Missile | ~80 | Best homing of any missile. Short range, wide lock-on arc. If it misses, turns around to re-engage. Fire-and-forget. |
| **SAAM** | Semi-Active Air-to-Air Missile | High | Player must keep target in crosshair after firing for continued guidance. Very high damage, strong homing while guided. Sniper weapon. |
| **LAAM** | Long-Range Air-to-Air Missile | Moderate | Extreme range. Limited homing. Good for BVR (beyond visual range) engagements. Fires in salvos of 2. |
| **HCAA** | High-Capacity Air-to-Air Missile | 63 | Fast reload, large ammo pool. Standard homing. Supplements standard missiles with slightly higher damage. |
| **SASM** | Short-Range Aerial Suppression Missile | Moderate | Proximity-detonation burst missile. Damages targets on near-misses. Effective against agile aircraft that dodge direct hits. |
| **4AAM** | 4-Target Air-to-Air Missile | 80 | Locks 4 targets simultaneously (or 4 on 1 target). Moderate range. Most versatile multi-lock. |
| **6AAM** | 6-Target Air-to-Air Missile | 80 | Locks 6 targets simultaneously. Extended range, weaker homing vs agile targets. |
| **8AAM** | 8-Target Air-to-Air Missile | 80 | Locks 8 targets. Can stack multiple on one target. Poor homing but massive volume. |
| **MSTM** | Multiple-Launch Standard Missile | — | Fires 4 standard-type missiles in rapid succession. Can target air or ground. XFA-27 exclusive (100 rounds). |

## Air-to-Ground Weapons

| Abbrev | Full Name | Description |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| **LAGM** | Long-Range Air-to-Ground Missile | Guided, wide blast radius, long range. Good for clustered ground targets. Low armor penetration. |
| **LASM** | Long-Range Air-to-Surface Missile | Anti-ship focused. High power, armor-penetrating. Sea-skimming flight profile. |
| **LACM** | Land Attack Cruise Missile | Massive damage, very long range. Slow reload, low ammo. Best single-target ground damage. |
| **4AGM** | 4-Target Air-to-Ground Missile | Locks 4 ground targets. Top-attack flight profile (climbs then dives onto target). |
| **8AGM** | 8-Target Air-to-Ground Missile | Locks 8 ground targets. Same top-attack profile as 4AGM. |
| **UGB** | Unguided Bomb | Free-fall, no homing. Wide blast radius. Manual pipper aiming. One-shots tanks. |
| **GPB** | Guided Penetration Bomb | Homing guided bomb. Small blast radius but devastating single-target damage. Armor-penetrating. |
| **SFFS** | Self-Forging Fragment Sub-Munitions | Unguided cluster bomb. Fragments into shrapnel at altitude. Drop height controls spread area. Can 1–2 shot non-boss ships. |
| **SOD** | Stand-Off Dispenser | Long-range guided glide bomb. Releases bomblets along flight path over target area. |
| **FAEB** | Fuel-Air Explosive Bomb | Thermobaric weapon. Massive blast radius. Lower per-hit damage than UGB but enormous area. ADF-01/F-15 S/MTD exclusive. |
| **XSDB** | Advanced Small Diameter Bomb | Homing bombs locking 4 ground targets. Higher capacity than missiles, focused damage. |

## Energy & Exotic Weapons

| Abbrev | Full Name | Damage | Description |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **TLS** | Tactical Laser System | Sustained | Continuous-fire laser beam. Instant hit, cannot be evaded if aimed accurately. Dispersed by clouds. Charge-up before firing. Air and ground capable. |
| **EML** | Electromagnetic Launcher | 120 | Railgun. Unguided, extremely fast projectile. Manual aim only. No homing. Devastating one-shot potential. |
| **PLSL** | Pulse Laser | Per-burst | Rapid-fire laser bursts. 5,000m+ range. Cloud-dispersed. Acts like an improved machine gun with splash damage. |
| **MGP** | Machine Gun Pod | Per-round | Two GSh-23L gunpods on wing hardpoints. Stacks with standard GUN for massive DPS. MiG-21bis exclusive (3,600 rounds). |
| **RKT** | Rocket Launcher | Per-rocket | Unguided rockets in rapid succession. No homing. Fragments on impact for area damage. |
| **GRKT** | Guided Rocket Launcher | Per-rocket | Guided rocket salvo (6 rockets per volley). Moderate range ~4,000m. F-104C exclusive (20 rounds). |
